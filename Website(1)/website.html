Jared Newman

Tech Journalist

I’m a freelance technology journalist with more than a decade of experience writing news, product reviews, analysis, and reported features. From my remote outpost in Cincinnati, I cover consumer devices and the services that tie them together, mainly for PCWorld, TechHive, and Fast Company. I've also written for The New York Times, Vice Motherboard, TIME.com, Macworld, Popular Mechanics, and Rolling Stone. My weekly column about cord-cutting has been running for more than five years, and my weekly cord-cutting newsletter has more than 20,000 subscribers.

Advisorator

Advisorator is my weekly newsletter and advice service covering all things tech. Every Tuesday, subscribers get an email full of practical tips and insights on phones, computers, apps and more. The service also includes periodic deal alert emails and advice on-demand via email or Advisorator's live chat room. Head to the Advisorator website for more details.
